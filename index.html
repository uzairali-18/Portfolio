How I Help Websites Rank

I focus on practical SEO work that improves search visibility without gimmicks. My approach is simple: understand what people search for, structure content properly, and make sure Google can read and rank the site.

1. Keyword Research

I find keywords people actually search for. Not vanity terms. I look at search intent, competition level, and what makes sense for the site's content. Tools I use: Google Keyword Planner, Ubersuggest, and manual SERP analysis.

2. On-Page SEO

I optimize the parts of the page Google reads: title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure (H1, H2, H3), and internal linking. I make sure the content answers the search query clearly and that the structure makes sense for both users and crawlers.

3. Content Optimization

I write and optimize content based on keyword research and search intent. The goal is to match what users are looking for, not just stuff keywords. I keep it natural, structured, and useful.

4. Basic Technical SEO

I handle the technical basics: mobile responsiveness, page speed optimization, clean HTML structure, proper use of semantic tags, and making sure the site is crawlable. I also set up Google Search Console and submit sitemaps.
